# Pricing Rules & Guidance

## Monthly Charges Guidance
- MonthlyCharges < 50:
    - Customer is price-sensitive but low revenue
    - Avoid discounts unless high churn risk
- MonthlyCharges between 50 and 80:
    - Balanced customer
    - Use targeted offers (support trial, contract upgrade)
- MonthlyCharges > 80:
    - High revenue customer
    - Consider discounts up to policy limits (max 15%)

## Total Charges Guidance
- TotalCharges is usually higher when Tenure is high
- If Tenure is low but MonthlyCharges is high:
    - Customer may feel value is low
    - Recommend improving service quality or giving short-term discount

## Payment Method Risk
- Electronic check:
    - Higher churn risk
    - Recommend switching to auto-pay methods:
        - Credit card (automatic)
        - Bank transfer (automatic)
