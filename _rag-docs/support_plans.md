# Support Plans & Add-ons

## Tech Support
Tech Support includes:
- Faster resolution time
- Dedicated support channel
- Setup and troubleshooting help

## When to recommend Tech Support
Recommend Tech Support if:
- TechSupport is "No"
- AND InternetService is "Fiber optic"
- AND MonthlyCharges > 70

## Device Protection
Device Protection includes:
- Hardware coverage
- Replacement options

## Streaming Services
StreamingTV and StreamingMovies improve engagement.
Recommend a streaming trial if:
- StreamingTV = "No" OR StreamingMovies = "No"
- AND customer is ActiveMember = false
