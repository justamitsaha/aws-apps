# Contract Terms (Simplified)

## Month-to-month
- Highest churn risk category
- Customers can cancel anytime
- Retention strategy:
    - Encourage moving to 1-year contract
    - Use small discount or contract upgrade bonus

## One year
- Medium churn risk category
- Customers are more stable
- Retention strategy:
    - Focus on support quality and add-on value
    - Avoid heavy discounts unless MonthlyCharges is very high

## Two year
- Lowest churn risk category
- Customers are stable
- Retention strategy:
    - Avoid discounts
    - Focus on satisfaction improvements (support, device protection)
